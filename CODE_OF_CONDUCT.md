# 📋 Code of Conduct

## 🎯 Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- 🌍 **Geographic location**
- 🎭 **Personal identity** (real or anonymous)
- 🔍 **Research methodology** (academic or investigative)
- 🏛️ **Political beliefs** (if relevant to research)
- 💻 **Technical skill level**
- 🎓 **Academic background**
- 🌐 **Language and communication style**

## 📜 Our Standards

### ✅ Positive Behavior

- 🤝 **Respectful Collaboration**: Work together respectfully
- 📚 **Evidence-Based**: Base discussions on verifiable information
- 🔍 **Critical Thinking**: Question and analyze constructively
- 🛡️ **Security Conscious**: Protect sensitive information
- 📝 **Clear Documentation**: Provide clear, accurate information
- 🎯 **Focused**: Stay on topic and relevant to conspiracy research
- 🔒 **Privacy Respect**: Protect identities when requested

### 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- ❌ **Harassment**: Threats, abuse, or personal attacks
- 🚫 **Doxxing**: Sharing private information without consent
- 💰 **Commercial Exploitation**: Using research for financial gain
- 🎭 **Impersonation**: False representation of investigators
- 📢 **Spam**: Irrelevant content or self-promotion
- 🔥 **Flame Wars**: Engaging in hostile arguments
- 🚫 **Illegal Content**: Instructions for illegal activities
- 📸 **Unverified Claims**: Presenting theories as facts without evidence

## 🔍 Research-Specific Guidelines

### 📚 Evidence Requirements
- 🔍 **Verifiable Sources**: Provide links to original sources
- 📊 **Documentation**: Include screenshots, files, or transcripts
- 🔄 **Corroboration**: Multiple independent sources preferred
- 📅 **Timestamps**: Include dates for temporal context
- 🌍 **Geographic Context**: Specify relevant locations

### 🛡️ Security Protocols
- 🔒 **Sensitive Data**: Use encrypted channels for sensitive information
- 🎭 **Anonymity**: Respect anonymous contributors and sources
- 📸 **Evidence Handling**: Store evidence securely
- 🚨 **Emergency Planning**: Have protocols for researcher safety

### 🏛️ Academic Standards
- 📝 **Proper Attribution**: Credit original researchers and sources
- 🔗 **Link Preservation**: Archive important links and documents
- 📊 **Methodology Transparency**: Explain research methods when possible
- 🔄 **Peer Review**: Encourage community verification
- 📅 **Update Maintenance**: Keep information current

## 🚨 Enforcement

### 📋 Reporting Process
1. **🐛 Create Issue**: Use "conduct" label for violations
2. **📧 Private Report**: Email maintainers for sensitive matters
3. **📸 Provide Evidence**: Include screenshots, links, or context
4. **🎯 Be Specific**: Detail which guidelines were violated
5. **📅 Timeline**: Allow reasonable time for response

### ⚖️ Consequences

#### 🟡 First Offense
- 📝 **Warning**: Private message explaining violation
- 📚 **Education**: Point to relevant guidelines
- 🔄 **Opportunity**: Chance to correct behavior

#### 🟠 Second Offense
- ⏸️ **Temporary Suspension**: 30-day restriction from participation
- 📝 **Formal Warning**: Documented violation record
- 🎯 **Specific Requirements**: Clear conditions for reinstatement

#### 🔴 Third Offense
- 🚫 **Permanent Ban**: Removal from community participation
- 📋 **Documentation**: Record of all violations
- 🚫 **No Appeal**: Final decision for severe violations

### 🛡️ Emergency Actions
- 🚨 **Immediate Ban**: For illegal activities or threats
- 🔒 **Content Removal**: Delete violating content immediately
- 📢 **Community Notification**: Alert community to serious violations
- 🚔 **Legal Reporting**: Report serious illegal activities to authorities

## 🤝 Community Guidelines

### 🎯 Research Focus Areas
- 🕵️ **Investigator Profiles**: Documenting legitimate researchers
- ⛓️ **Supply Chain Security**: Technical analysis and tools
- 🛡️ **Victim Support**: Resources for targeted individuals
- 🏛️ **Academic Research**: Evidence-based studies and papers
- 🛠️ **Tool Development**: Security and investigation tools

### 📝 Contribution Standards
- ✅ **Quality**: High-quality, well-researched content
- 🔍 **Verification**: All claims should be verifiable
- 📚 **Documentation**: Clear, comprehensive descriptions
- 🔗 **Link Quality**: Working, relevant links only
- 🏷️ **Proper Tagging**: Use appropriate labels and categories

### 🌐 International Considerations
- 🌍 **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect different cultural contexts
- 🏛️ **Legal Awareness**: Understand jurisdictional differences
- 🌐 **Language Accessibility**: Clear, accessible language
- 📅 **Time Zone Awareness**: Consider global participation

## 📞 Contact Information

### 👥 Community Moderators
- **Primary**: Repository maintainers
- **Backup**: Trusted community contributors
- **Advisory**: Security researchers from network

### 📧 Reporting Channels
- 🐛 **Public**: GitHub issues with "conduct" label
- 🔒 **Private**: Encrypted email (available on request)
- 🚨 **Urgent**: Direct message to maintainers
- 📊 **Feedback**: Regular community surveys and discussions
